Investoria Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 16, 2025
Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how Investoria collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our city-building investment platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

Information Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, email, phone number, date of birth Account creation and identity verification
Financial Information Investment preferences, risk tolerance, financial goals Personalized investment experience
Investment Data Portfolio holdings, transaction history, performance Platform functionality and visualization
Usage Information Game progress, preferences, feature usage Service improvement and personalization

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Location Data: General location for regulatory compliance (not precise location)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, authentication tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Enable account access, investment visualization, and platform features
  • Process Investments: Facilitate investment transactions through our broker-dealer partners
  • Personalize Experience: Customize your city-building investment journey
  • Communicate: Send account updates, educational content, and service notifications
  • Ensure Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Comply with Regulations: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns and enhance platform functionality

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers and Partners

  • Broker-Dealers: Licensed partners who execute investment transactions
  • Technology Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and security services
  • Customer Support: Third-party support and communication tools
  • Marketing Partners: With your consent, for relevant financial services

Legal and Regulatory Requirements

  • Compliance with securities laws and regulations
  • Response to legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protection of rights and safety of users and the public
  • Prevention of fraud and illegal activities

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, subject to confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security and Protection

Security Measures:
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive financial data
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance for data handling
  • Employee background checks and security training

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal information
  • Update: Correct or update your account information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications (investment updates remain)
  • Choose frequency of educational content delivery
  • Manage push notification preferences

State-Specific Rights

If you are a resident of California, Virginia, Colorado, or other states with privacy laws, you may have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login and preferences
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account remains active
  • Required by law or regulation (typically 7 years for financial records)
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes
  • Needed to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses for international transfers
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Certification under recognized privacy frameworks

9. Children's Privacy

Investoria is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with external services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external services you use.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • Prominent notice on our platform
  • Push notification (if enabled)
